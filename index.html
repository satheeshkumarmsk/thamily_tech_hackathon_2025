At a Hackathon, you'll join a team of like-minded students/professionals, each bringing their unique talents to the table. You'll collaborate, brainstorm, and tackle real-world challenges in Tamil using technology. Whether you're a seasoned coder or just starting out, there's a place for you here. It's not just about writing code; it's about thinking outside the box, solving problems, and pushing the boundaries of what's possible.

Anyone can form a team and participate. Identify the problem statement that you want to solve. Minimum Team Size should be 2 maximum 4. submit you idea through registeration form. We will screen and shortlist the potential and original ideas. Shortlisted participants will be notified and encouraged to attend our Bootcamps in StartupTN Madurai regional hub. Finals and valedictory will be conducted in Madurai. Remote participations are allowed for finalists only from other states/countries.

Promote Innovation in Tamil Language Technology: Foster the development of innovative solutions addressing challenges in Tamil language processing, translation, and digital engagement, paving the way for language-centric startups.
Enable Startup Opportunities: Encourage participants to transform their ideas into viable startup ventures by leveraging Tamil language technology to create impactful products and services.
Bridge Academia and Industry: Connect university students, researchers, and tech professionals with startup founders and industry leaders to encourage collaboration and the translation of academic research into entrepreneurial opportunities.
Encourage Product Development: Support the creation of prototypes and minimum viable products (MVPs) during the hackathon that can be refined and scaled as startups addressing Tamil language challenges.
Enhance Ecosystem Collaboration: Bring together startups, investors, mentors, and ecosystem stakeholders to create synergies, enabling knowledge sharing and investment in Tamil language tech startups.
Empower Regional Entrepreneurs: Provide a platform for aspiring entrepreneurs from Tamil-speaking regions to innovate in the language tech domain and contribute to the growth of the regional startup ecosystem.
Promote Social Impact through Technology: Develop tech solutions that preserve and promote Tamil language, culture, and heritage, creating a broader societal impact while building commercially viable ventures.
